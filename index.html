About Us

University Chicken was born out of an earnest desire to help students find a quick, affordable meal that tastes how a warm hug feels.

Each sandwich helps our community bring education, awareness to food insecurity, and create opportunity through the power of food.

By employing local college students University Chicken fosters independence by building trust in every student, parent, faculty member, and guest we graciously serve. Contributing a percentage of annual sales to local non profits strengthens our bonds, commitment, and sincerity to being the change we want to see. Purpose and passion drive everything we do.
