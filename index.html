About Us

University Chicken was born out of an earnest desire to help HBCU students find a quick, affordable meal that feels like a warm hug. Everyone can relate to hunger, and no one should face food insecurity. Every sandwich helps our community by bringing education, awareness, and opportunity through the power of food. By employing local college students, University Chicken fosters independence and trust in every student, parent, faculty member, and guest we graciously serve.
