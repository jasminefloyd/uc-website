Meet the Chefs

University Chicken was born out of an earnest desire to help HBCU students find a quick, affordable meal that tastes how a warm hug feels. Everyone can relate to hunger, and no one should face food insecurity. Every sandwich helps our community bring education, awareness, and opportunity through the power of food. By employing local college students, University Chicken fosters independence and trust in every student, parent, faculty member, and guest we graciously serve.

Contributing a percentage of sales annually with our non profit partner partner True Blue 1881, strengthening our bonds, commitment, and sincerity to being the change we want to see.
